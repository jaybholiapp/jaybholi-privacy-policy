# Privacy Policy for Jay Bholi (जय भोलि)

**Last updated:** 27 June 2026

Jay Bholi ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is an offline-first shop management
application for retail and wholesale businesses in Nepal. This Privacy Policy
explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Jay Bholi, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Who we are

Jay Bholi is operated by the Jay Bholi team ("the developer").

For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at:

**Email:** jaybholicustomerservice@gmail.com

---

## 2. Information we collect

We collect the following information so the App can function. We only collect
what is needed to run your shop and your account.

### Account information
- **Name** — your display name and the names of customers and suppliers you add.
- **Email address** — used to create and sign in to your account (email/password
  or Google Sign-In).
- **Phone number** — your phone number and the phone numbers of customers and
  suppliers you add.
- **Address and area information** — optional customer, supplier, or shop
  address details you enter, such as address, village/area, and ward.
- **User ID** — a unique account identifier generated by our authentication
  provider (Firebase Authentication).

### Business and financial information
- Sales, purchases, payments, expenses, credit/ledger balances, inventory, and
  related records you enter to manage your shop. This information is private to
  your account.

### Photos
- Product images, receipt scans, and expense receipt images that you choose to
  capture or upload.

### Contacts
- When you choose to add a customer or supplier from your phone's contacts, the
  App reads your contacts so you can select one. **Only the contact you select
  (name and phone number) is saved.** We never upload or store your entire
  address book.

### Messages and content
- Messages and order details exchanged between shops and customers/suppliers
  through the App's ordering features.

### Device identifiers
- A push notification token (a device identifier) so we can send you
  notifications about orders, payments, and account activity.

We do **not** collect your location, browsing history, health data, or audio.
We do **not** use advertising identifiers or show third-party ads.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account and authenticate you securely;
- Provide the core features of the App (inventory, sales, customers, suppliers,
  orders, payments, expenses, and reports);
- Sync your data across your devices and back it up so you don't lose your
  records;
- Enable communication between shops and their customers/suppliers;
- Send you notifications related to your account and transactions;
- Maintain the security and reliability of the App.

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** share your personal
information with third parties for advertising or marketing.

---

## 4. How your information is stored and protected

Your data is stored locally on your device and synced to secure cloud services
provided by **Google Firebase** (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and
Cloud Storage). Firebase acts as our data processor.

- All data transmitted between the App and our servers is **encrypted in
  transit** using industry-standard TLS/HTTPS.
- Access to your business data is restricted to your authenticated account.

You can learn more about how Google handles data in the
[Firebase Privacy and Security documentation](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
and the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 5. Sharing of information

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

- **Service providers** — We use Google Firebase to host, authenticate, and sync
  your data on our behalf.
- **Within the App** — When you connect with a shop, customer, or supplier, the
  information necessary to complete an order (such as your name, phone number,
  address or area information where needed, and order details) is shared with
  that connected party so the transaction can be fulfilled.
- **Legal requirements** — We may disclose information if required to do so by
  law or valid legal process.

---

## 6. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete
your account, we delete your personal data and business records associated with
your account, except where we are required or permitted to retain limited
information for legal, tax/accounting, security, fraud prevention, dispute
resolution, backup, or compliance purposes. Backup copies may take up to 90 days
to be fully removed from our systems.

---

## 7. Your rights and choices

You can:

- **Access and update** your information from within the App.
- **Request deletion** of your account and associated data at any time.

To delete your account and data, visit our
[account deletion request page](https://jaybholiapp.github.io/jaybholi-privacy-policy/delete-account.html)
or email us at **jaybholicustomerservice@gmail.com** and we will process your
request after verifying account ownership.

---

## 8. Children's privacy

Jay Bholi is intended for business owners and is not directed at children under
the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has
provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.
